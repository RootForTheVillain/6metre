Why 6 Metre?

metre (noun) /me·ter/

Why 6Metre Financial? 'Why Not?' The inspiration for the firm name stems from the 6 Metre class of racing sailboat.  'Why Not,' which sailed in the 1948 Olympics, is now owned and captained within the family.

Setting Sail.

The 6 Metre class is known for its agility, performance, and adaptability to conditions.  The vision for this firm, founded upon similar principles, was to create a highly sophisticated, client centered financial services firm that leverages industry leading technology to deliver comprehensive financial strategies.

Navigation.

The financial services industry and retirement planning landscape can be incredibly complex and confusing.  Knowing that you have a trustworthy professional that has your best interests at heart is vital. 6Metre Financial leverages an open architecture platform to connect you with the most suitable financial instruments (from a broad range of companies) to fit your needs and accomplish your goals.  Transparency and thorough explanation are paramount to forging deeply seeded trust and long lasting client relationships.
