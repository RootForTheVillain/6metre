Why 6 Metre?

metre (noun) /me·ter/

Why 6Metre Financial? 'Why Not?' The inspiration for the firm name stems from the 6 Metre class of racing sailboats.  'Why Not?' was built in Sweden in 1947 and competed in the 1948 Olympics.  After a well-traveled history, including tenure in New York, the vessel is now owned and captained within the family.

Setting Sail.

The 6 Metre class is known for its agility, performance, and adaptability to conditions.  The vision for this firm, founded upon similar principles, was to create a highly sophisticated, client centered financial services firm that leverages industry leading technology to deliver comprehensive financial strategies.

Navigation.

The financial services industry and retirement planning landscape can be incredibly complex and overwhelming. Knowing that you have a trustworthy professional that has your best interests at heart is vital. 6Metre Financial utilizes an open architecture platform, connecting you to an extensive range of companies and products to find the most suitable financial instruments that fit your needs and accomplish your goals.  Transparency and thorough explanation are paramount to forging deeply seeded trust and long lasting client relationships.
